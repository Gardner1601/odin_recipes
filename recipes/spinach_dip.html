Spinach Dip Recipe

Description

This delicious spinach artichoke dip is so cheesy and fragrant. This dish can be can be the perfect appetizer for Date night, or a great snack to enjoy with your friends.
You can also enjoy it by yourself...I have.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounces) package of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of grated Romano cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon of garlic salt
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (14 ounces) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1/2 cup frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Lightly grease small baking dish
  2. Mix cream cheese, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, Romano cheese, garlic, basil, garlic salt, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
  3. Gently stir in artichoke hearts and spinach
  4. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish; top with mozzarella cheese
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and lightly browned, about 25 minutes.
